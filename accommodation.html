B&B accommodation

If you have a taste for authentic country life and want to escape city sophistication for a rural environment, then Summerhill Farm may be just what you want.


As a lived-in working property (much like a farm stay in feel) and a little off the main tourist drives, Summerhill Farm presents the quiet face of the Peninsula. Even mobile coverage is limited, so you really can get away from it all.
