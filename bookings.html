Bookings

Check in:

In general, check in from 2pm and check out before 11am. If you advise an approximate arrival time, I can get either a heater or air conditioner going for your room, if the weather is particularly hot or cold.


Additional beds:

Additional charges apply if extra beds are needed. Please advise if this is the case.


Deposit required:

Upon booking, a deposit (50% of the total value of your stay) is required to confirm your reservation.

Cancellations and refunds:

We understand that life happens and plans change. Please ring and advise - we may be able to find another date for your visit, if appropriate.


If you cancel more than 21 days prior to reservation date, your deposit will be refunded less a $10.00 administration fee.


If notice is given 7 to 21 days prior to your booking, 50% of the deposit will be refunded.


If less than 7 days notice is given, no deposit will be refunded.
