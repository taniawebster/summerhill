The Blue Cottage

Secluded in its own private corner of the rambling country garden, The Blue Cottage (circa 1940s) is relaxed, unpretentious and comfy - perfect for country escape.

The Blue Cottage consists of a queen bedroom with en-suite, sitting/dining room and small kitchen. It includes a TV and CD player, a wood heater and alpaca throw for chilly evenings and a heated towel rail for chilly mornings. Breakfast ingredients supplied for you to cook your own English-style breakfast sufficient for two mornings; bring your own fresh ingredients for additional meals.


The small terrace is simply furnished with chairs and table and an electric BBQ.


A porta-cot and a fold-up single mattress (within the ottoman) are available. Additional fold-out beds can be supplied - please ask.


A kennel is available for when your dog is on holiday with you. Well-behaved dogs are permitted in the Blue Cottage.
