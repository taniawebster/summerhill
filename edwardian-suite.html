The Edwardian Suite consists of a Queen bedroom with en-suite and a spacious sitting room with TV and CD player. An alpaca throw adds a luxurious touch.


Ingredients are provided for a generous Continental-style breakfast (cereal, toast, juice, yogurt and fruit). No cooking facilities but a fridge, microwave, kettle and toaster are available.


For an extra charge the Rose Room (another queen bed and a fold-out double sofa) can be included with the Edwardian Suite, allowing accommodation for two couples or extended family. Additional cots and fold-out beds can be supplied - please ask.


Pets are not allowed inside the Edwardian Suite.
