Wedding Photos


The gardens and paddocks at Summerhill Farm are also available for wedding photography.

If you are looking for a rural backdrop for your special requirements, we offer both the garden and paddocks (with or without curious animals) for $200/hour.  Bookings are essential.  Enquiries and inspection are welcome.  

Over the years Summerhill Farm has been the location for wedding photos, an Australian ABC TV drama and promotional videos for fashion apparel. Click on the following links for a sample of the photos and films shot at Summerhill Farm.
