Near Us

Only a 75 minute drive from Melbourne CBD, we are close to beaches, wineries & restaurants, golf courses, fromageries, craft markets, high-ropes courses, national parks, tea rooms, galleries and beautiful display gardens.

Whether immersing yourself in the farm or enjoying the many and varied local attractions, relax and unwind in our lovely area.

Here are some of our favourite local places.  Please ask if you have any questions.  Local transport options if you don't want to drive yourself include the Ridge Rider Shuttle Bus and MP Designated Drivers.

Red Hill Estate

A multi-award winning winery with breathtaking views. Their Pinot Noir is the best in Australia.

Mock Red Hill

Apple orchard where visitors can sample a range of apple ciders, juices and freeze-dried fruits.

Peninsula Horseback Tours

Experience a winery tour like no other, or just have a fantastic day out with family, friends or collegues.

 T'Gallant

The birthplace of Pinot Gris and Grigio wines in Australia. Their cellar door and pizzeria are well worth a visit.

Sunny Ridge Strawberry Farm

Pick your own strawberries or buy freeze-dried berries, handmade jams, candy and more.

Bass and Flinders Distillery

Here you can buy locally produced gin, spirits, liqeuers and brandy, tour the distillery, or do a Gin Masterclass. 

Johnny Ripe Bakery

Eat-in or take away - fresh orchard produce and daily baked goods, with different manu in each season.

Main Ridge Dairy

Take a tour of this farmhouse cheese factory and goat dairy and taste its handcrafted cheeses.

Arthurs Seat Enchanted Maze

An adventure wonderland and the home of tree surfing.

Gordon Studio Glassblowers

An art glass gallery and studio, featuring hand blown creations by the local glass artists.

Montalto

Eat at the piazza cafe or the restaurant, sample Montalto wines, or enjoy your own private picnic with a view.

Peninsula Hot Springs

Immerse yourself in natural thermal mineral waters which flow from deep underground.
